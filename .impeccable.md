# Design Context - TimorList

## Users

- **Primary**: Timor-Leste local community (Timorese, expats, NGOs, businesses, government agencies)
- **Use cases**:
  - Find local businesses (restaurants, shops, services)
  - Discover government services and public sector info
  - Connect with NGOs and non-profits
  - Browse and list classified ads (jobs, products, property, vehicles)
  - Businesses promote products & services via SKU system
- **Context**: Mobile-first (Dili traffic, limited connectivity), mix of urban/rural users
- **Job**: Discover and connect with local businesses, government, NGOs, and services

## Brand Personality

- **Voice**: Vibrant, cultural, local-first
- **Tone**: Celebrating Timor-Leste identity, welcoming, community-focused
- **3 words**: Vibrant, Cultural, Local
- **Emotional goal**: Pride in local businesses, trust in community
- **Reference**: Gumtree-style listings + professional business directory hybrid
- **Anti-references**: Generic corporate directory, cold/sterile feel

## Aesthetic Direction

- **Theme**: Dual (Light/Dark) - default **Light**
  - Light: Cream background (#FDFBF7) + Yellow primary (#FFD150)
  - Dark: Zinc dark (#09090b) + Yellow primary (#FFD150)
- **Style**: Warm, welcoming - NOT overly corporate, NOT playful - Vibrant & Cultural
- **Typography**: Inter (body) + Oswald (headings) - Local fonts
- **Spacing**: Tailwind scale (4px base unit)
- **Border radius**: `rounded-xl` (cards), `rounded-md` (inputs/buttons)

## Design Principles

1. **Local pride**: Celebrate Timor-Leste businesses and culture, not generic Western patterns
2. **Trust & clarity**: Clear information hierarchy, honest listings, no dark patterns
3. **Warmth**: Yellow/cream evokes warmth and approachability
4. **Fast & functional**: Quick loads, simple forms, efficient search and browse
5. **Mobile-first**: 44px touch targets, thumb-friendly navigation, works on slow connections

## Card Display (2026-05-11 Enhanced)

| Entity | Badge | Key Info |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Business | Industry (yellow bg) | Title (bold), Address (pin), 5-star rating (filled/empty), Likes (heart), Views (eye) |
| Non-Profit | Category (rose bg) | Title (bold), Address (pin), Likes (heart), Views (eye) |
| Public Sector | Category (blue bg) | Title (bold), Address (pin), Likes (heart), Views (eye) |
| Listing | Type + Price (color-coded) | Title (bold), Location (pin), Price (yellow bg), Likes (heart), Views (eye) |

**Card Design (2026-05-11)**:
- Aspect ratio 4:3 for images
- Pagination: 12 items/page
- Hover: yellow border (`border-primary/40`) + shadow lift
- `rounded-2xl` border radius
- **Image background**: `bg-gradient-to-br from-primary/10 to-primary/5` (brand yellow tint)
- **Title**: `font-bold` + `line-clamp-2`
- **Address**: `text-sm` with `w-4 h-4` pin icon
- **Rating**: Full 5-star display (filled amber / empty gray)
- **Stats**: `w-3.5 h-3.5` icons, `gap-4` spacing
- **Listing type colors**: Job (blue), Product (emerald), Service (purple), Property (amber), Vehicle (red), Wanted (teal)
