# Design Context - TimorList

## Users

- **Primary**: Small business owners in Timor-Leste (Dili-focused)
- **Secondary**: Tourists, expats, locals searching for local businesses
- **Context**: Mobile-first browsing, often on slower connections
- **Job**: Discover local businesses, products, government services, NGOs

## Brand Personality

- **Voice**: Professional but approachable, local-first
- **Tone**: Warm, trustworthy, community-focused
- **3 words**: Yellow, clean, trustworthy
- **Emotional goal**: Confidence in finding reliable local businesses

## Aesthetic Direction

- **Theme**: Warm yellow (#FFD150) on cream (#FDFBF7)
- **Style**: Clean, modern, professional - NOT overly corporate, NOT playful
- **Typography**: Inter (body) + Oswald (headings) - Google Fonts
- **Spacing**: Tailwind default scale (4px base unit)
- **Border radius**: `rounded-xl` (cards), `rounded-md` (inputs/buttons)

## Design Principles

1. **Yellow brand identity**: Primary color (#FFD150) used consistently
2. **Clean whitespace**: Generous padding, breathing room
3. **Trust signals**: Verified badges, ratings, reviews visible
4. **Fast & accessible**: Lighthouse-friendly, WCAG AA compliant
5. **Mobile-first**: Works perfectly on mobile devices
